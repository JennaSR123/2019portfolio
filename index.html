ABOUT ME

I'm a 22 year old graduate from Massachusetts Bay Community College, with an AS in Computer Information Systems and certificates in Web Design and Cyber Security. I've been making websites since I was 14 years old, when I posted "Hello, World!" for the first time. Ever since then, I was hooked, and I've been picking up new tricks as I go. I love working in Bootstrap, and there's nothing so satisfying as making a smooth CSS animation.

I started shooting photography when I was fifteen years old. I was taught the basics of it in a high school course, and then brought what I learned to summer camp and ran a photography merit badge when I was sixteen. Check out some of my work below.

In my spare time, I'm a barista at the local Starbucks. I can be found behind the bar, or maybe in my favorite spot with my laptop... designing websites. You can also find me out in the woods with my camera on a sunny day. Besides that, I might show up to karaoke night, sometimes.
