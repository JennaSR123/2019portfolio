Home

Welcome to Picturesque Photography!

Picturesque Photography is a phofessional photography business in the New England area, with specializations in landscapes and portraits, especially in formal events and cosplay.

PP's last event was at Anime Boston in the Hynes Convention Center, Boston, MA. Check out the gallery below!

About

Jenna is a young college student at Massachusetts Bay Community College. Her interest in photography started in high school through a vocational school program which introduced her to many different forms and mediums of art. She began taking photos professionally after a photography course taught her how to frame her images, and began teaching it to others while working at an arts center in Bolton, MA.

Jenna's hobbies in costuming, exploring, and cooking allow her to shoot many subjects casually.

Jenna's favorite subjects include beautiful scenery, her friends, home-cooked food, and local cosplayers.
